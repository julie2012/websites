About Us

Nestled amidst the lush greenery and breathtaking landscapes of the mountains, Calawag Mountain Resort offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Our resort is a sanctuary where nature and comfort meet, providing guests with the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. At Calawag Mountain Resort, we pride ourselves on creating unforgettable experiences for our guests. Whether you're seeking tranquility in our cozy accommodations, exploring scenic hiking trails, or indulging in local delicacies, we ensure every moment is filled with warmth and hospitality. Our commitment to preserving the beauty of our surroundings is reflected in our eco-friendly practices and sustainable tourism initiatives. We aim to be a haven for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and families looking to reconnect with nature and each other.
